Art Title 1

Medium: Digital Media & Oil on Canvas

Year: Media 2021

Dimensions: 39.5*39.5cm

Description: 走不出的肠道 DIGITAL Those Intestines U Could Never Walk Through 哺乳动物的肠道是尤为重要的，大量的消化作用和几乎全部消化产物的吸收都是在小肠内进行的，大肠主要浓缩食物残渣，形成粪便，再通过直肠经肛门排出体外。 世界像一个巨型的情绪加工厂，循环往复，只让我们吸收、不让我们排泄。昏暗里我们发着微弱的光，发出求救信号的“我们”不得已矫揉在一起。当生物在放大下被细细观察，一览无余，被看得体无完肤。随之加工，瓦解，冰消。 The intestinal tract of mammals is particularly important. A large amount of digestion and almost all of the absorption of digestive products takes place in the small intestine, which concentrates food residues into feces and then passes through the rectum and anus. The world is a giant emotion processing factory: going and returning in a circle, letting us absorb rather than excrete. We glow faintly in the gloom, and are forced to knead together as we send out distress signals. When living things are scrutinized under a microscope, they are seen all over the place. Processing, disintegration, deicing…
